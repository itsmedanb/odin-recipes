Lasagna Recipe

The Best Lasagna Ever

The best lasgna you will ever eat.

All you need to do is follow this recipe.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound italian sausage
  • 3/4 pound lean ground beef
  • 1/2 cup minced onion
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 1 can crushed tomatoes
  • 2 cans tomato paste
  • 2 cans canned tomato sauce
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons dried basil leaves
  • 1/2 teaspoon fennel seeds
  • 1 teaspoon italian seasoning
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 tespoon blacl pepper
  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 16 ounces ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 3/4 pound mozzarella cheese
  • 3/4 cup parmesan cheese

Steps to cook

  1. Combine all ingredients in a bowl
  2. Mix thuroughly
  3. Place mixed ingerdients into a baking pan
  4. Pre heat over to 375
  5. Bake for 2 hours, or until golden brown
