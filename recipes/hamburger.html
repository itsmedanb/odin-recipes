Hamburger Recipe

The Best Hamburger You've Ever Had

Without a doubt the best hamburger you've ever had. All you have to do is follow these simple instructions.

ingerdients

  • Hamburger bun
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • sliced tomatoes
  • Sliced Onions
  • Lettuce
  • Condiments

Steps To Cook

  1. cook hambuger paties on barbeque for 7min each side.
  2. Toast hamburger buns
  3. Add mayo to the bottom and top bun
  4. place hamburger patty on bottom bun
  5. Place lettuce, tomatoes, and onions on top of the hamburger patty
  6. put ketchup on top of vegetables
  7. Put top hamburger bun on vegies
