BLT Recipe

The Best BLT You've Ever Had

Without a doubt the best BLT you've ever had. All you need to do is follow these simple instructions.

Ingredients

  • Sliced white bread
  • Bacon
  • Sliced Lettuce
  • Sliced Tomatoes
  • Mayo

Steps To Make

  1. Cook bacon
  2. Toast bread
  3. Spread mayo across both bread slices
  4. Put letuce on bottom slice of bread
  5. Put tomato on top of the lettuce
  6. Put bacon on top of tomato
  7. Put other slice of bread on bacon
  8. Enjoy!
